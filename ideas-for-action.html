Ideas for Action Stories, and Pics!

DnD stories that have action, can help keep your players entertained and focused on the task you have laid out before them.

Set up Scenes

  • Villian's apperance

    • Great way for getting everyone on egde.

  • Chase encouter

    • Forces your players to act quickly, and sometimes not think about the consequences of their actions.

  • Intial fight, before the brawl

    • Creating a great fight intro can keep people hooked with the session.

  • Group attack

    • Allowing the players to plan their attacks as one is always satisfying.
