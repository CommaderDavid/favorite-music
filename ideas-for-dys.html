Here are some ideas for creating Dystopian stories

Dystopian worlds can create intresting scenarios for your players. Like, what do you do when you must make choices for yourself, instead of others?

List (and Pics) for Scenarios

  • The Beginning of the End

    • Great way to introduce the players to your ruined world.

  • The New World

    • Make the players feel like they are stepping onto an alien world.

  • Lost hope

    • Don't be afraid of making your players feel the dispair of a dead world. Just don't make it always feel like that. It is only a game after all.

  • New Dangers

    • This is where the worlds of Dystopias thrive. There are new and horrific scenarios for you to use against your players.
