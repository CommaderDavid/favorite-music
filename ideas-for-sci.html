Ideas for Sci-fi Worlds

Sci-fi is a theme that you can use to create worlds entirely unique to your players.

Best types of Worlds to make, in order!

  1. Original

    2. Creating an Original world is a great way to get your players into your game. Having something they never have seen makes each session fresh and exciting!

  2. Future

    3. Future worlds are a place where many limitations we have are able to be removed for convenience of the players.

  3. Distiant

    4. Worlds that are distiant can give them a sense of being isolated, and can give the players a somewhat unnerved feeling.

  4. The Unknown

    5. Creating an unknown universe for your players to play in, forces them to try and survive in a world they know nothing about.
